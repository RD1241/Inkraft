What is the Character Vault?

The Vault stores your characters' core visual traits (hair color, outfit, facial features). When generating a comic, check their profiles under Active Cast to force the AI consistency engine to keep them looking identical panel-to-panel!

1. Create: Define a character name and visual description here. 2. Activate: Check their profiles under Step 4 (Active Cast) in the generator workspace. 3. Render: The AI automatically overrides scene extraction descriptions with your Vault sheets!
